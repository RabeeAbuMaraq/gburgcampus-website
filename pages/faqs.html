FAQs
Updated April 2024
How do I download the app?
You can download the app from the App Store using the button on the homepage.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, the Gettysburg Campus App is free for all students, faculty, and staff.
What devices are supported?
The app is available for iOS and Android devices. Please check the App Store or Google Play for compatibility.
Who can I contact for support?
If you need help, please use the contact form on our website or email support@gettysburg.edu.
Does the app collect any personal data?
No personal data is collected by the Gettysburg Campus App. Please see our privacy policy for more details.
Have more questions?
If you have any additional questions, do not hesitate to contact us.
