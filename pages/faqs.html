Campus v2.1
Frequently Asked Questions
Updated July 2025
1. What is the Gettysburg Campus App and who is it for?
The Gettysburg Campus App is a student-designed mobile platform built for Gettysburg College students, faculty, and staff to easily access campus services, schedules, events, and resources in one place.
2. How do I log into the app?
You can log in using your Gettysburg College email credentials. Authentication is handled securely through the college’s Single Sign-On system.
3. Is the app free for all students and faculty?
Yes, the app is completely free to download and use for all members of the Gettysburg College community.
4. Can I view my class schedule and assignments through the app?
Yes. The app includes a personalized dashboard where students can view their daily schedule, upcoming classes, and assignment summaries.
5. Does the app include campus dining menus and meal balance?
Absolutely. You can view current dining hall menus, opening hours, and check your Bullet Bucks and meal swipe balances in real time.
6. Will I receive notifications for events and class changes?
Yes. You can opt in to receive real-time notifications for upcoming events, academic reminders, class cancellations, and alerts from campus services.
7. Can I use the app to navigate the campus or locate buildings?
Yes. The interactive campus map helps you locate buildings, departments, shuttle stops, and key campus landmarks.
8. How often is the information in the app updated?
Information is updated daily, with real-time integrations for schedules, dining, and events provided by the college’s APIs and data sources.
9. Is there a dark mode or theme customization?
Yes. Users can switch between light and dark mode, and even choose from several minimalistic themes for a personalized experience.
10. How can I provide feedback or request support for the app?
You can use the “Feedback” option in the More tab to submit comments, report bugs, or request new features. We’re actively listening!
Have more questions?
If you have any additional questions, do not hesitate to contact us.
